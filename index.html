FAQ
Common Questions
Everything you need to know before you start.
Is Romieaux free to use?
+
Yes. The Explorer tier is completely free and includes 8 core modules: Activities, Dining, Transportation, Stays, Safety & Prep, Payment Checklist, Memory Book, and Reminders. No credit card required. Upgrade to bundles or All-Access anytime for premium intelligence.
How does group pricing work?
+
Only the Trip Owner needs a subscription. When you create a trip and invite friends or family, your subscription covers intelligence for everyone on that trip. Invitees create free accounts and get full access to the trip's itinerary, polls, budget alignment, and all active modules.
What's the difference between bundles and All-Access?
+
Bundles ($9.99/mo each) group modules by travel style — Explorer, Smart Traveler, Family, Solo, Couples & Celebrations. All-Access ($29/mo or $299/yr) unlocks every module and all 18 intelligence engines. If you travel frequently or with different group types, All-Access is the best value.
Can I cancel anytime?
+
Yes. All subscriptions are month-to-month with no long-term commitment. Cancel anytime from Settings. Your data and Travel Blueprint are always yours — they persist even on the free tier. Annual plans can be cancelled with a prorated refund.
Does Romieaux book travel for me?
+
Romieaux is a travel intelligence platform, not a booking engine. It designs your experience, organizes your workflow, and provides direct links to book through your preferred providers. You always control where and how you book — Romieaux makes sure every decision is informed.
What is the Travel Blueprint?
+
Your Travel Blueprint is a persistent identity profile that captures how you travel — energy level, trip style, budget preferences, dietary needs, music taste, and more. It evolves across trips as Romieaux learns what works for you. Every recommendation is shaped by your Blueprint, not generic data.
Can I travel with pets?
+
Yes. Toggle "Traveling with a pet?" during onboarding to activate free pet-friendly filtering across stays, transport, dining, and activities. The Pet Travel module (Couples & Celebrations Bundle) unlocks deeper intelligence including breed-specific airline restrictions, quarantine rules, and Dogpack integration.
Is my data secure?
+
Romieaux is built security-first. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We follow SOC 2, GDPR, and CCPA compliance standards. Your Travel Blueprint and trip data are never sold to third parties. You can export or delete your data at any time from Settings.
